What to Expect During the Long-Term Care Insurance Underwriting Process
We have completed the first stage of applying for Long-Term care insurance - completing your application. 
• When you have reviewed and signed your application, we will submit to the application for the underwriting process to begin.
• You are submitting the application(s) pre-paid. This means that the Conditional Insurance Agreement goes into effect and that agreement protects your insurability while in underwriting. If you decide to not take the policy, your premiums paid will be refunded to your bank account.
• The Conditional Insurance Agreement is an agreement, in consideration of prepayment of an application, that operates to protect the insured’s insurability as of the Stated Date for underwriting purposes under certain conditions.

PLEASE REFER TO THE CONDITIONAL INSURANCE AGREEMENT THAT IS PART OF THE APPLICATION FOR MORE DETAILS.
• Within 24-48 hours of submission of your application you will see a draft in the amount of $[insert amount] from the bank account you authorized. 
An underwriter will be assigned to review your application(s) and will begin to send us updates throughout the process. Other underwriting requirements may be needed: 
• Medical Records from your primary care physician, as well as other healthcare providers.
• Personal History Interview (PHI) this used as a supplement to the information on your application and includes a cognitive assessment conducted by a medical professional over the phone.
• Responses to questions the underwriter has that will assist in the completion of the underwriting process.
We will make every effort to make sure the application process goes efficiently and smoothly, and we will keep you updated throughout the process. 
Please note that underwriting can generally take up to 4-6 weeks and delays sometimes occur. 
The most common delay involves obtaining medical records. Other potential delays can occur if we have difficulty contacting you for follow-up questions or completing the Client History Interview (CHI) in a timely manner. If we need your help processing your application, we will reach out.
